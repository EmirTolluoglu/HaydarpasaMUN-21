The third session of Haydarpaşa MUN was held between 28-31 January 2018 in Haydarpaşa High School. The executive team
consisted of; Secretary General Harun Özcan, Deputy Secretary Generals İbrahim Yusuf Mert and Mehmet Polat, and İpek Aydınhan,
Director General Tutku Öztürk and Deputy Director Generals Feride Can and Buse Merter. Total number of 450 delegates
attended 11 committees which were:

  • Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)
  • United Nations Human Rights Council
  • International Atomic Energy Agency
  • International Maritime Organization
  • United Nations Security Council Counter Terrorism Committee
  • International Criminal Police Organization (INTERPOL)
  • Joint Crisis Committee
  • Historical Security Council
  • Napoleon's Cabinet
  • Philadelphia Congress
  • International Court of Justice
