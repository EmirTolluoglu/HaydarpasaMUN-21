Cabinet of MEHMET I

Introduction

X

  A state with 4 different claimants… Hostilities and bloodshed among brothers and 11 years of poverty and chaos. The Ottomans and their magnificence had taken a huge blow in this Period of Interregnum.
In the year 1402 Bayezid, I lost The Battle of Ankara and was held captive in the hands of Amir Timur. Western and Central Anatolian lands were plundered and burned down by the Timurids. Timur didn’t want anything left strong or resumable from the Ottomans to do that he wanted to set against claimants of the throne with each other. Civil war broke out among the sons of Sultan Bayezid I; Şehzade Süleyman, Şehzade İsa, Şehzade Musa and Mehmed for the throne.
For this year the HaydarpasaMUN delegates will decide the fate of the Ottomans. As the cabinet of Mehmed, our goal in this committee is to make our Sultan Mehmed one and only ruler of Devlet-i liyye Osmaniye and make Devlet-i liyye SIGNIFICANT again…
