Venue

Transportation

The conference will be held in our school building which is in Koşuyolu, Üsküdar. Participants coming from:

1) Üsküdar; can take Koşuyolu minibuses or buses coded 11, 11A, 11C, 11D, 11E, 11EK, 11G, 11K, 11L, 11M, 11N,
11P, 11ST, 11Ü, 11ÜS, 11Y, 12ÜS, 139, 2, 9ÜD and get off at Altunizade station.From there, school is within a 15 minutes walking distance.

2) Kadıköy; can take Koşuyolu minibuses and get off at Koşuyolu İSKİ. From there, school is within a 5 minutes walking distance.

3) Marmaray, Üsküdar, Ümraniye; You can use the Üsküdar-Ümraniye metro line to Altunizade station to our school. You need to walk for 7 minutes to arrive our venue.

4) Sabiha Gökçen Airport; You can get on the bus E-10 to metrobus line Uzunçayır station. You need to use metrobus
line to the Altunizade station. By walking 10 minutes you can arrive our venue.

5) Istanbul Airport; You can get on the bus M1A to Marmaray line Yenikapı Station. You need to get off the
Marmaray in Üsküdar station then interchange to the Üsküdar-Ümraniye metro line. You can get off the metro from Altunizade station. After walking for 7 minutes you will be arrived to our venue.

Participants who wish to use metrobus should get off at Altunizde station. From there, school is within a 15 minutes
walking distance.
