The first session of HPALMUN was held between 6-8 April in Haydarpaşa High School. The Executive team consisted of Secretary General
Selin Balay, Deputy Secretary General Elif Nur Ilguz,Director General Alptekin Dayı, Deputy Director General Merve İçin. Total number of
240 delegates attended 8 committees which were:

  • SOCHUM
  • Organisation of Islamic Cooperation
  • Stalin's War Cabinet
  • Shanghai Cooperation Organization
  • JCC: Dissolution of the Soviet Union (1991)
  • Ad Hoc Committee of the Secretary General on Cyprus Dispute
  • Futuristic Military Comittee of NATO
  • Turkey-Eu High Political Dialogue Committee
